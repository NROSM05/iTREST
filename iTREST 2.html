Meet Yassin Guiding Young Minds to Shine Hi, my name is Yassin, and I’m not just a consultant—I’m a kid who understands kids! At iTrest, I focus on helping young children discover their strengths, overcome challenges, and unlock their full potential. What makes iTrest unique? I’m one of them. Whether it’s building confidence, improving focus, or developing new skills, my mission is to guide young minds to shine. Let’s work together to make their future brighter.
